Watering Method

  • Water root zone, not leaves — from beneath foliage
  • Gentle flow — no splashing (dislodges seeds)
  • Seedlings & cuttings: water at pot edge, never blast
  • Johnsons pump: open bypass valve into tank FIRST
  • Check rose fitting isn't sun-hot — run briefly first

Pest Watch (while watering)

  • Rats — check gabion guards, love sweet shoots
  • White butterflies — eggs on brassicas, check caterpillars
  • Spiders — olive trees especially; heavy = kills tree
Nettle tip: Dried nettle layer on pots = watering indicator. When it lightens, pot needs water.
5 senses check first: LOOK (wilting?), LISTEN (pump OK?), SMELL (ammonia = overwatering), TOUCH (soil moisture), TEMPERATURE (thermometer).
