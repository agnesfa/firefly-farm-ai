🏙️ Guest Guide
Shower, dry toilet & water supply
Welcome to Firefly Corner! This guide covers the three shared camp facilities. Tap each section below for step-by-step instructions.
💧 Water Supply ▶
1.Open the main tank valve — located on the tank near the shower entrance.
2.Switch on the water pump — at knee height, right side of the shower entrance.
Conservation: We have one tank and low rainfall. Please use water sparingly — short showers, turn off taps between use.
🚽 Dry Toilet ▶
Daily Use
1.Use the toilet as normal.
2.Cover with straw from the container — stored under the hinged panel on the left.
3.1 scoop after pee, 2 scoops after poo.
The straw cover material keeps things dry and odour-free. Always cover after use.
When the Bucket is Full
1.Lift the hinged seat panel from the front.
2.Remove the bucket carefully.
3.Carry to the creek paddock.
4.Empty into the green compost compound (the right-hand one).
5.Return bucket and replace the seat panel.
🚿 Shower ▶
Cold Shower (Default)
1.Locate the white control box on the left wall.
2.Follow the blue hose to the orange connector.
3.Turn the black switch to start the water flow.
Warm Shower (Optional)
1.Open the gas bottle valve — turn anti-clockwise.
2.Then turn the black switch on the orange connector to start flow.
Do not adjust the black knob settings on the white shower box.
After Use
1.Close the gas valve (if used) — turn clockwise.
2.Turn off the black switch on the orange connector.
3.Switch off the water pump.
