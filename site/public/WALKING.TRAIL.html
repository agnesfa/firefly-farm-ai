Before you head out

  • Wear closed shoes — paths can be uneven and grassy.
  • Bring water, a hat, and sunscreen.
  • Please leave gates as you find them.
  • Stay on the marked route — working paddocks, syntropic rows, and the nursery are out-of-bounds unless you’re invited.
  • Dogs welcome on lead; please pick up after them.
  • Snakes are occasional in warmer months — stay on clear ground and step carefully around logs.
